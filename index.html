Studio Ghibli

Studio Ghibli Inc. (Japanese: 株式会社スタジオジブリ) is a Japanese animation studio headquartered in Koganei, Tokyo. It is best known for its animated feature films, and has also produced several short subjects, television commercials, and two television films. Its mascot and most recognizable symbol is a character named Totoro, a giant spirit inspired by raccoon dogs (tanuki) and cats[3] from the 1988 anime film My Neighbor Totoro.
